Exhibiting at Anime Expo offers various opportunities - whether you
want to launch a new product, showcase your upcoming releases, or
just get your name out there!

Get your name in front of tens of thousands of anime fans.

From Exhibit Hall to Entertainment Hall to lobby activations, Anime Expo offers various locations for companies to exhibit. Connect with us determine the best fit based on your goals, your content, and your needs.



Exhibit Hall

Primarily focused on sales, the Exhibit Hall spanning over 340,000 square feet (that’s about the same size as six football fields!). Attendees traverse the hall in search of clothing, collectibles, toys, and more.


  • Booth sizes start at 10′ x 10′
  • Certain restrictions and requirements apply for exhibitors with prop weapons or mature content.

    • Entertainment Hall

    Focused on cosplay and gaming, the Entertainment Hall is a curated space featuring interactive exhibits such as gallery displays, free play area, demo stations, and more.


  • Booth sizes start at 25′ x 25′
  • Sales activities are limited due to the interactive focus of the space.
  • Open Thursday through Saturday from 10 AM to 10 PM and Sunday from 10 AM to 4 PM

    • Lobby Activations

    Be among the first activations that attendees interact with by exhibiting in premium spaces located in the South and West Lobby areas of the convention center.


  • Restrictions apply depending on location
  • Hours vary depending on location



    • Amenities

    Info Services

    Look out for Info Services booths throughout the Los Angeles Convention Center for help with any questions such as where things are located, when things are happening, or how late certain parts of the convention are open until.


    Event Operations

    Visit Event Operations for Disabled Services or to file a report about an incident, a suspicious person, or a lost child/person. Lost children are escorted to Event Operations to await their parent or guardian. Event Operations is also where you can notify us about any problems, such as spills or broken things.


    Lost & Found

    If you have lost an item at Anime Expo, please visit the Lost & Found room located in West Hall, room 507. If you would like to turn in a lost item, you can drop it off at any Info Services Booth or the Lost & Found room. After Anime Expo, lost items are turned over to Baguio Convention Center Guest Services. Please contact them at +63 123456789. If you lost your item at the JW Marriott, please contact them at +63 123456789.
