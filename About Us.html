About us

Mission

To create a group that promotes positivity and inspires people to improve on their crafts - cosplay,

crafting, cosplay photography & videography, makeup, costume/character designing.

To bring together artists from all over the world and share their works with each other.

In the future, have regular on-ground group gatherings in respective countries, convention

meet-ups, workshops, collaborative photo and video shoots, features, giveaways/contests, community work, etc.

Vision

Setting differences aside for the love of cosplay.

No matter where you’re from, it’s our passion that unites us! A family that upholds and

promotes good conduct and support towards fellow artists/cosplayers. A venue where you

can safely share your works, connect with fellow artists and build relationships with.
